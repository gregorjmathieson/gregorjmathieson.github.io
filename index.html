Language Progress
This is a little project of mine to aid in my language learning. The idea is that I will post writing and speaking exercises each week or so in order to practice my skills in each language, as well as have something to look back on and see any progress and improvement. This is also the first website I will have designed by myself, so it is a little experiment to see how good I am at web design.
